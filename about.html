The REdecorator Lucy Smith

 

I am Lucy Smith aka, “The REdecorator”. Let me cast an experienced eye over your room to transform it using your own furnishings and accents. I have over 35 years of interior design and color knowledge to help pull your walls, floors, furniture and accessories together for a pleasing, comfortable fit with your lifestyle and pocketbook.

I live in an 1840’s Damariscotta Cape that my husband and I have been lovingly restoring.

I spent over 25 years in the custom interior fabrication business, from owning and operating a workroom to being top sales and designer for a high end furniture store.

Let me work with you to come up with the home, room, accent you will be pleased with and be proud to call yours.
